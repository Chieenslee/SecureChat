1. Gioi thieu bai toan

Ung dung chat van ban qua mang co nguy co bi nghe len, sua doi, gia mao nguoi gui va gui lai goi tin cu. De tai yeu cau xay dung ung dung bao mat tin nhan bang AES-256-CBC, RSA-2048 va SHA-256. He thong can dam bao tinh bi mat, tinh toan ven, xac thuc nguoi gui va co phan hoi ACK/NACK khi packet hop le hoac bi loi bao mat.

Du an hien tai xay dung ung dung chat that qua WebSocket relay. Moi nguoi dung mo mot tab rieng, tu sinh khoa RSA, dang ky public key len server, gui handshake va message packet qua server trung gian.

3. Muc tieu bao mat

3.1. Tinh bi mat

Noi dung tin nhan duoc ma hoa bang AES-256-CBC truoc khi truyen. Server khong nhan plaintext va khong nam giu khoa AES.

3.2. Tinh toan ven

Receiver tinh lai:


SHA-256(IV || ciphertext)

Neu hash tinh lai khac hash trong packet, receiver tu choi va gui NACK.

3.3. Xac thuc

Sender ky metadata va payload message bang RSA/SHA-256. Receiver dung public key cua sender de verify signature.

3.4. Anti-replay

Moi message co session_id va sequence_number. Receiver luu sequence number da xu ly. Neu packet cu bi gui lai, receiver phat hien va NACK.

3.5. Packet het han

Moi packet co timestamp. Ung dung tu choi packet co timestamp qua cu de mo phong yeu cau het han/sai quyen trong huong dan bo sung.

7. Thiet ke giao thuc

7.1. Handshake


sequenceDiagram
    participant A as Nguoi dung 1
    participant S as Relay
    participant B as Nguoi dung 2

    A->>A: Tao RSA keypair
    B->>B: Tao RSA keypair
    A->>B: Trao doi public key
    A->>A: Tao AES session key
    A->>A: Ky metadata UserID + session_id
    A->>A: Ma hoa AES key bang public key cua nguoi nhan
    A->>S: Handshake packet
    S->>B: Relay handshake
    B->>B: Verify signature va giai ma AES key

7.2. Gui message


sequenceDiagram
    participant A as Nguoi dung 1
    participant S as Relay
    participant B as Nguoi dung 2

    A->>A: AES-256-CBC encrypt
    A->>A: SHA-256(IV || ciphertext)
    A->>A: RSA sign payload
    A->>S: Message packet
    S->>B: Relay packet
    B->>B: Check timestamp
    B->>B: Check replay
    B->>B: Verify hash
    B->>B: Verify signature
    B->>B: AES decrypt
    B->>A: ACK/NACK

Thu tu xu ly tai receiver:

Kiem tra timestamp.

Kiem tra replay bang sequence number.

Kiem tra hash.

Kiem tra chu ky.

Giai ma AES.

Hien thi message va gui ACK.

12. Han che va huong phat trien

Han che con lai:

  • Public key distribution mac dinh tin tuong server.
  • Log server hien luu in-memory, chua co database.

Huong phat trien:

  • Luu security log tren server.
  • Them database public key fingerprint.
  • Them QR/fingerprint public key de giam MITM.
  • Them export benchmark thanh file.
  • Mo rong che do WebSocket de ho tro phong chat nhieu nguoi va luu log dai han.

13. Ket luan

Du an da xay dung duoc ung dung chat bao mat chay that qua WebSocket relay theo de tai 16. Lop RSA dung node-forge de bam sat RSA-2048 PKCS#1 v1.5 + SHA-256; AES-CBC va SHA-256 duoc xu ly bang Web Crypto API.
